Short Bio

Jialong Li received his B.E. degree in Electronic Engineering from Tsinghua University in 2016, where he is currently pursuing the Ph.D. degree. He is advised by Prof. Xiaoping Zheng and Prof. Bingkun Zhou. His research interests include optical networks and optical communications.
